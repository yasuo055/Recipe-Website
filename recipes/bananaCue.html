Banana Cue

Description

This is a popular Filipino street food made with deep-fried plantains coated in brown sugar. It's a simple yet satisfying snack. You just need saba bananas (plantains), brown sugar, and cooking oil for frying.

Ingredients

  • Saba bananas (plantains)
  • Brown sugar
  • Cooking oil
  • Skewers

Steps

  1. Prepare the bananas
  2. Heat the oil
  3. Caramelize the sugar (optional)
  4. Fry the bananas
  5. Coat with sugar
    • Method 1
      • If you caramelized the sugar in a separate pan, remove the bananas from the oil and immediately toss them in the caramelized sugar until coated.
    • Method 2
      • If you didn't caramelize the sugar separately, remove the bananas from the oil and sprinkle them with the remaining brown sugar while they are still hot. Use a spoon or tongs to coat them evenly.
  6. Skewer the bananas
  7. And last, Serve!
