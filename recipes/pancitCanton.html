Pancit Canton

Description

This is a stir-fried noodle dish that's easy to customize with your favorite protein and vegetables. You can use store-bought egg noodles, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and any combination of vegetables, chicken, pork, or shrimp.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Oil
  • Aromatics:
    1. 1 Small onion
    2. Diced 3 cloves garlic
    3. Minced
  • Protein
    1. Thinly sliced pork belly or chicken (around ½ pound)
  • Vegetables
    1. Julienned carrots (1 cup)
    2. chopped green cabbage (1 cup)
    3. sliced snap peas (½ cup)
  • Seasoning:
    1. ¼ cup soy sauce
    2. 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
    3. salt
    4. ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the noodles
  2. Marinate the protein(optional)
  3. Sauté the aromatics
  4. Cook the protein (optional)
  5. Add vegetables
  6. Incorporate the sauce
  7. Add the noodles
  8. Garnish and serve!
