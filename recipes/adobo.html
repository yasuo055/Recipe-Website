Chicken Adobo

Desctription

Filipino Chicken Adobo is the national dish of the Philippines and like all traditional dishes, there are many variations â€“ including different proteins like pork and beef.

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Soy sauce
  • White vinegar
  • Onion and garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Sugar
  • Bay leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Sear the chicken
  3. Saute the aromatics
  4. Simmer with marinade
  5. Add chicken and simmer
  6. Reduce and thicken the sauce
  7. And last, Serve!
